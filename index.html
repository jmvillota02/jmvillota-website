Go to Middle SectionThe Philippine Arena, situated in Bocaue, Bulacan, is a monumental multipurpose indoor arena and a striking testament to architectural innovation. Owned by the Iglesia ni Cristo, a prominent religious organization in the Philippines, the arena boasts an awe-inspiring design highlighted by its iconic dome-like roof. With an extraordinary seating capacity of approximately 55,000 to 60,000 individuals, it ranks among the world's largest indoor venues. Its flexible layout accommodates an array of events, from international concerts and sports competitions to religious gatherings and conventions. Beyond its architectural grandeur, the Philippine Arena holds profound cultural and religious significance, often serving as the backdrop for vital occasions in the Iglesia ni Cristo's calendar.
